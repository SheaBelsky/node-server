SpoilRator

Natural Language Understanding in your refrigerator!

About Us

SpoilRator is an intuitive home assistant meant to keep track of food found in one's refrigerator, detect what food is fresh and spoiled, and present this information to fridge raiders.

Using Android Things, a camera takes a picture every time the fridge door is opened. This image is then sent to a Node.js server, which sends it to Google Cloud Platform's Visual Recognition service.

Visual Recognition returns information about the image, including what foods are present. Combined with a MySQL database, Node.js delivers metrics on how long the food has been sitting there, and what foods might have gone rotten. Furthermore, food that has been removed from the fridge will be removed from the database, so you'll always be up to date with what's being kept cold!

All of this information is delivered by a touch-screen display that sits outside the refrigerator.
